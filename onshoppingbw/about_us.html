About Us

OnShopping BW

OnShopping BW is an online marketplace designed to connect customers with sellers across Botswana in a simple and efficient way. Our platform helps customers save time and energy by allowing them to browse and search for products from different sellers in one place instead of visiting multiple stores.

Once customers find a product they are interested in, they can directly connect with the seller through WhatsApp to discuss details, negotiate, and complete their purchase conveniently. OnShopping BW only facilitates the connection between customers and sellers; therefore, we do not take responsibility for payments, deliveries, or any transactions that take place between buyers and sellers.

We provide an opportunity for individuals and businesses to showcase and sell their products online. To support new sellers, OnShopping BW offers free registration with unlimited product uploads for the first 50 businesses for a period of three months. After this period, sellers pay an affordable subscription fee of P50 per month to continue using the platform.

Our goal is to create a reliable and accessible digital marketplace that supports local businesses while making shopping easier for customers. Sellers may list a wide range of products on the platform, provided that all items comply with legal standards and regulations.
